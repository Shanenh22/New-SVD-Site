Dental Cleanings & Exams in North Dallas

The foundation of everything else, gentle professional cleanings, honest comprehensive exams, and zero judgment about how long it's been.

Medically reviewed by Dr. Nicole Houshmand, DDS · Updated June 2026

5.0 · 214 Google reviews Caring for North Dallas families Most PPO plans accepted

Most patients need a professional cleaning and exam every six months. Each visit includes scaling, polishing, a gum measurement, digital X-rays as needed, and a thorough exam. Cost for uninsured patients: $150–$300. Most PPO plans cover two cleanings per year at 100%.

How often do you need a dental cleaning and exam?

Almost every expensive dental problem starts as a cheap one. A cavity caught at a six-month exam is a small filling; the same cavity found two years later is a root canal and a crown. Early gum inflammation reverses with a cleaning and better home care; established periodontitis is a lifelong management project. Regular cleanings and exams aren't the boring part of dentistry, they're the part that makes the rest of it mostly unnecessary.

At Spring Valley Dental, a hygiene visit is never an assembly line. Your hygienist works gently and explains as she goes, and one of our two dentists, the same two you'll see at every visit, performs the exam personally, shows you what they see on screen, and gives you a straight answer about what matters and what doesn't.

Been a while? You will not be lectured here. Whether it's been six months or sixteen years, the visit starts the same way: we look, we explain, we plan. Most patients who've been away for years tell us the dread was far worse than the appointment.

What happens during a professional dental cleaning?

Professional cleaning, plaque and tartar removal, polishing, and flossing
Comprehensive exam of teeth, gums, bite, and existing dental work
Digital X-rays when needed, low radiation, instantly on screen
Gum health measurement to catch periodontal disease early
Oral cancer screening at every comprehensive exam
An honest priority list, what needs attention, what can wait, what it costs

Why prevention beats repair

Insurance plans cover preventive visits generously for a reason: they save everyone money. Most PPO plans cover two cleanings and exams per year at or near 100%. Compare the math, a covered cleaning versus a crown, an implant, or gum surgery, and the six-month visit is the best deal in healthcare. No insurance? Our membership plan exists for exactly this: cleanings, exams, and X-rays for a flat annual fee, plus discounts on other treatment.

What if it's been years since your last dental visit?

First, the honest version: if it's been a long time, your first visit may take longer than a routine recall, and if tartar has built up below the gumline we may recommend a deep cleaning (scaling and root planing) rather than a standard one. We'll show you exactly why on the imaging, explain the difference, and put the cost in writing before anything begins. What won't happen: guilt, pressure, or a treatment plan designed around our schedule instead of your budget. And if anxiety is the real barrier, Dr. Houshmand is certified in conscious sedation, read how that works.

Cleanings & exams, FAQ

For most adults, a professional cleaning and exam every six months keeps plaque, tartar, and early problems under control. Some patients, those with gum disease history, heavy tartar buildup, or certain health conditions, benefit from three- or four-month intervals. We recommend a schedule based on what we actually see, not a one-size-fits-all rule.
