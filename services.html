One office. One team. Every stage of your dental health.

Spring Valley Dental Associates provides family, cosmetic, restorative, and emergency dentistry, including in-house dental implants and conscious sedation, at 14228 Midway Rd, Suite 100, Dallas, TX 75244.

Most dental problems don't arrive one specialty at a time, a cracked tooth becomes a root canal, becomes a crown; years of putting off visits become gum treatment, a deep cleaning, and a plan to rebuild confidence in your smile. That's why Spring Valley Dental Associates is built as a true full-service practice. General, cosmetic, restorative, and emergency dentistry are handled in the same office by the same two dentists you'll see at every visit.

For you, that means no referral ping-pong for most procedures. Dr. Aletaha places and restores dental implants in-house. Dr. Houshmand is certified in conscious sedation, so anxious patients can have treatment completed comfortably here rather than being sent elsewhere. Root canals, extractions, veneers, Invisalign, gum therapy, one team carries your history from the first exam through the final result.

And whatever brings you in, the philosophy is the same: no judgment about how long it's been, honest explanations of what you actually need (and what you don't), and your costs explained clearly before any work begins. Browse the services below, or book a visit and we'll help you figure out where to start.

Not sure where to start?

Match your situation to the right first step

In pain right now, or something broke? Skip everything else and go to emergency dentistry or just call (972) 852-2222. We hold same-day slots open every day we're open.
It's been years since your last visit? Start with a comprehensive exam and cleaning. No lectures, we meet you where you are, assess honestly, and build a plan in order of what actually matters.
Missing one or more teeth? Read about dental implants and bridges, then book a consultation. We'll give you a straight comparison of options, longevity, and realistic costs.
Unhappy with how your smile looks? Whitening handles color, bonding handles small chips and gaps, veneers transform shape and proportion, and Invisalign handles alignment. Most smile makeovers combine two of these.
Anxiety is the real obstacle? You're not alone, and you won't be judged here. Read about sedation dentistry and how we work with anxious patients many tell us their first visit back was easier than years of dreading it.
