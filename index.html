Why patients choose us

Above and beyond is just how we do things.

Spring Valley Dental Associates is a bilingual (English/Spanish) family and cosmetic dental practice in North Dallas, open until 7 PM on Mondays and Wednesdays, with same-day emergency appointments held open every business day.

Patients tell us we're the first dentist's office they actually look forward to. Here's why.

No judgment. We've seen it all.

2 years? 12 years since your last visit? We don't count. We help, no lectures, no guilt. Come as you are and we'll take it from there. Our patients consistently say this is the thing that surprised them most.

We explain every cost before we start.

We work with 30+ insurance plans and review every cost and option with you before a single thing begins. The bill will never surprise you. If something changes, we tell you before we proceed, every time.

In pain? We'll get you in today.

Same-day appointments for dental emergencies, every day. Dr. Houshmand has personally met patients at the office outside normal hours when they needed help. That's not marketing, that's who we are.

Dental anxiety? We have real options.
